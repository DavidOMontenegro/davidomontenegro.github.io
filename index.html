The Dev Lab

Hello, world! I'm David, a backend developer with a passion for building creative digital experiences. This site is my home on the web: a place where I share my projects, write about what I’m exploring, and occasionally release small games just for fun.


I built this site because I wanted something more personal and intentional than scattered GitHub repositories or half-finished notes. I wanted a place where I could organize my projects, document what I’m learning, and give meaningful context to the things I build. If you’d like a deeper explanation of why this site exists and what I’m hoping to do with it, I wrote an extended introduction in my first blog post: Welcome to The Dev Lab.


Feel free to reach out or connect with me on LinkedIn or GitHub, especially if you have feedback about the site or job opportunities.
